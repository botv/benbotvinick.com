Chestnut Hill Meals on Wheels

Donate

Your donations help keep our doors open. Chestnut Hill Meals On Wheels relies on contributions from the local community to support our home-delivered meals program.

Volunteer

Volunteering at Chestnut Hill Meals on Wheels can be a very rewarding personal experience. Volunteer drivers do more than deliver a hot, nutritious meal. They also provide a valuable confirmation that our recipients are safe that day.

Apply

A simple phone call to Chestnut Hill Meals on Wheels office at (215) 233-5555 is all it takes to apply for service.

Why Meals on Wheels?

Meals on Wheels delivers the support that keeps seniors in their homes, where they want to be.

  • Seniors say it improves their health
  • Clients say it enables them to remain living at home
  • Clients say it makes them feel more safe and secure
